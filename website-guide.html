AgroAssist - User Guide

What is AgroAssist?

AgroAssist is a digital platform designed to empower farmers with technology-driven solutions for better agricultural practices. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional farming and modern technology by providing essential tools and information at your fingertips.

Key Features:
  • Crop Disease Detection: Identify plant diseases using image recognition technology
  • Multilingual Support: Access the platform in your preferred regional language
  • Real-time Market Prices: Get updated prices for your agricultural produce
  • Weather Forecasts: Plan your farming activities with accurate weather predictions
  • Government Schemes: Stay informed about beneficial government programs
  • User Management: Register as a Farmer or Merchant to access all features

Getting Started

1
Account Registration

To access all features, you need to create an account:

  • Click on the Login button
  • Select Register option
  • Choose your role (Farmer or Merchant)
  • Fill in your details including name, mobile number, and address
  • Create a secure password
2
Logging In

After registration, you can login using your mobile number and password:

  • Enter your registered mobile number
  • Enter your password
  • Click Login to access your dashboard

Using AgroAssist Features

1. Crop Disease Detection

This feature helps you identify diseases in your crops by analyzing images:

1
Click on the Image-based Crop Disease Detection card
2
Click Upload Image to select a photo of your crop
3
Click Analyze Image to get results
4
View the analysis results and recommended treatments
Tip:

For best results, take clear photos of affected leaves or plants in good lighting conditions.

2. Multilingual Support

AgroAssist supports multiple Indian languages:

1
Click on the Multilingual Support card
2
Select your preferred language from the dropdown menu
3
The interface will automatically update to your selected language
3. Market Prices

Stay updated with current market rates for your produce:

1
Click on the Real-time Market Prices card
2
View the latest prices for various crops
3
Filter by crop type or region if available

Managing Your Profile

1
Updating Information

You can update your profile details anytime:

  • Find your profile in the right sidebar
  • Click the Update button
  • Edit your information in the popup window
  • Click Save to update
2
For Merchants

Merchants can update their product listings and prices:

  • Go to the Merchants section
  • Click Update on your listing
  • Edit your product details and prices
Best Practices:
  • Check the dashboard regularly for updates
  • Use the weather forecasts to plan irrigation and harvesting
  • Update your profile whenever your contact details change
  • Log out after each session if using a shared device

Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

Problem: Can't login

  • Ensure you're using the correct mobile number
  • Check your password is entered correctly
  • If forgotten, use the password reset option

Problem: Image analysis not working

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure the image is clear and properly focused
  • Try with a different image

Problem: Language not changing

  • Refresh the page after selecting language
  • Clear browser cache if issue persists
